# Workstation Onboarding Checklist (Concise)

## Pre-Provisioning
- Device registered in Autopilot  
- Assigned to correct group/profile  
- Windows Pro license assigned  

## Provisioning
- Autopilot deployment completed  
- Compliance policies applied  
- Defender onboarding validated  
- BitLocker escrowed  

## Post-Provisioning
- Apps installed  
- Email configured  
- Browser policies applied  
- User added to appropriate groups  

## Intent
Standardized onboarding flow ensuring identity, security, and compliance alignment.
